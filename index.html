What is Serenity?

Serenity Counseling & Psychological Services is a place for those who need mental support and psychological well being. Basically, we have in-personal counseling services and online counseling services as well. In the meantime, we are only providing online counseling service via video conferencing due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

More, we have been initiating many training sessions, for instance, Basic Counseling Techniques Training with two batches, Psycho-social Support Training with four batches and Mindfulness classes.
